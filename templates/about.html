Who We Are

Better Cities Start with Better Communication.

We built CivicPulse because we were tired of walking past the same broken streetlamp for weeks. We believe fixing your neighborhood shouldn't require endless phone calls or waiting in line.

View Source Code
The Problem

In growing cities, small problems turn into big messes simply because the right people don't know about them in time. A small water leak today becomes a burst pipe tomorrow. But traditional reporting systems are often slow, confusing, or just a black hole where complaints disappear.

Our Solution

CivicPulse is a simple bridge between residents and officials. We make it easy for you to snap a picture and report an issue in seconds. For officials, we cut through the noise and highlight the critical problems that need attention right now. It’s not just about data; it’s about getting things fixed.
