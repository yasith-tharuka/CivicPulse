Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

CivicPulse, We respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when using our infrastructure reporting platform.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Username and district location.
  • Report Data: Titles, descriptions, and categories of infrastructure issues you report.
  • Usage Data: Timestamps of when you log in or submit reports.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to facilitate the reporting and resolution of urban infrastructure issues. Your reports are visible to assigned district officials to enable repairs. We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.

4. Security

We implement standard security measures, including password hashing, to protect your account. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
